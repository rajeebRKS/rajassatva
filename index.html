About us

A legacy built on trust, craft, and detail.

Rajas Satva, a unit of Raajas India P. Limited, has spent three generations outfitting professional kitchens across the northeast and beyond. From the first consultation to perpetual after-sales support, we move with one promise — your kitchen runs, on day one and decade three.

What we do

Six disciplines, one accountable partner.

Whether you need a single fryer or a turnkey 5,000 sq.ft. kitchen, every category below is delivered, installed, and serviced by the same team.

Kitchen equipment

Cooking ranges, brat & boiling pans, deep-fat fryers, grills, refrigeration and processing equipment — manufactured in-house and sourced from Hoshizaki, Unox, Electrolux, Sirman and more.

Bakery solutions

Ovens, proofers, mixers, moulds, racks and display counters from Sinmag, Leenova, Silikomart and Sanneng — with custom fabrication for any layout constraint.

Tablewares & FOH

Bone china, porcelain, glassware, cutlery, chafing dishes and risers — curated from BP Bharat, Pasabahce, Ariane, Ikai Asai and Nivana by Ekaani.

Exhaust systems

On-site CFM analysis, custom hood and duct fabrication, and full installation — engineered by our draftsmen for clean, comfortable working environments.

Gas pipeline

Safe, code-compliant LPG and PNG pipeline design and installation, using only certified fixtures and fittings — backed by post-installation testing and AMC.

Consultancy & design

Kitchen layout planning, equipment specification, power and ventilation requirements — delivered as a complete drawing set our contractors can build from.
